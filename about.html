About Me

Studied IT at Demalin in 2017 but left school in 2018 because of financial difficulties. Despite being in my first year, I felt compelled to take advantage of every opportunity to learn as much as possible, so I applied for a tech internship. In the year that I left college, I was able to secure an internship as a junior web developer, during which I learned about php, vue js, angular dart, laravel and mysql, scrum, etc. The majority of our work was done in Vue JS front end and Laravel back end.

That continued until March of 2019, at which point it became difficult to find employment without a degree. Some may question why I never returned to school while working as an intern, but the R2,500 per month we received was really insufficient for me to make ends meet. I really enjoyed working in a development environment, so these days I just take any part-time job I can get to try and save money for my education. I don't see myself doing anything else.

Nonetheless I'm greatful for this opportunity I got and I'll definitely make the most of it.
